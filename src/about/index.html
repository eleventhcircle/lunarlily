Hello there, fellow explorers!

I'm Luna, the webmaster of lunarlily. I use they/them pronouns, and I'm in my late 20s. I love bunnies and studying the stars. Nice to meet you!

After some encouragement from a friend, I sat down to make this little place a reality. I'm not sure what it's going to be exactly, beyond somewhere that I can collect the things I like and maybe even my thoughts about them, but I'm looking forward to watching it evolve over time. Who knows what the future holds?

I'm a bit shy but you're always welcome to drop me an email or leave a message in the guestbook.
