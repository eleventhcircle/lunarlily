Thing of the Week

Week of April 5, 2026: Odyssey by Valiant Hearts

Posted April 6

When I was still using Spotify a few years ago, I stumbled across Dear Astronomer from this album and I'm very grateful I did. Valiant Hearts has a very... quintessential Luna sound, if that makes sense lol? Friends that I've shared music with before will probably get what I mean. My favorites on here are Like Ships in the Night, Colourwave, and Medusa, but this is one of those albums I can listen through on repeat all day no skips. Impatiently waiting for the next Bandcamp Friday so I can buy their newest release...
