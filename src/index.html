Thing of the Week

Week of July 19, 2026: these really cute bunny index tabs from Daiso

Posted July 19

They took my two favorite things (rabbits and stationery) and mashed them together. What was I supposed to do, NOT get them?

I'm slowly becoming someone who likes to tab up their favorite books, and that means I'm now in constant need of index markers that can withstand the test of time. I have three or four little packs that I picked up as a student, but they're all paper so they're a bit on the flimsy side... the adhesive on the really old ones barely works now too, but I can't bring myself to just throw them out. These bunny shaped ones from Daiso are plastic though, so they're durable against tears and bending! And like, look at them. They're so cute. Foolishly I only got one pack and I ran out after just three books...
