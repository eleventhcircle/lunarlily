Thing of the Week

Week of February 1, 2026: Doomer by Tokyo Manaka

Posted Feb 1, 2026

My bestie (if ur reading this hiii) dropped this in my DMs back in early December, so I suppose this is technically a Thing of the Last 2 Months? I've had it on repeat constantly since then, it kept me company through many of the evenings I spent working on the site update. Lo-fi beats to crash out over compile errors to or something idk

I like songs that make you feel like you're floating through the clouds (or like that one gif of Goofy in the pool) and Doomer hits that perfectly for me. This is the first song I've heard from Tokyo Manaka but I'm charmed by his sound, I want to listen through the rest of his releases soon. The MV is quite striking too! I spent a couple hours looking through ohuton's pixiv archive recently - I recommend taking a peek, their style is a lot of fun and they've even got a few manga you can read through.

All of this to say: if you haven't already heard it, it's worth a listen :] Lay back and dive~
