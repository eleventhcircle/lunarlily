Thing of the Week

Week of May 17, 2026: Tomodachi Life Toolkit

Posted May 20

While I'm sure Tomodachi Life itself needs no introduction in this part of the web, I thought I'd share some tools I've been using regularly alongside the game. None of these are mandatory for a fun play experience, of course! If they don't spark joy for you, no need to use them :]

  • TomoPlanner's Personality Calculator

    TomoPlanner started making the rounds on Bluesky after Uncle Nintendo dropped the game demo! I think it's a good tool if you're someone who wants to plan out exactly who will live on your island, especially if you have a big cast in mind. I'm not that type of player myself, but I do make ample use of the included personality calculator when I'm cooking up new Miis.

  • Living the Grid - Pixel art drawing helper

    Listen, I know what I'm good at and drawing ain't it. This has helped me get a couple custom items into the game with a lot less headache than if I had tried to fully freehand them.

  • TomoLingo - Island lingo generator

    Community sourced dictionary of island lingo ideas. If you're stuck on ideas when your Miis ask you for something, it's worth taking a peek! Be mindful that there's quite a lot of NSFW lingo lol but you can filter them out.
