Thing of the Week

Week of June 7, 2026: Baby's first Tamagotchi

Posted June 13

Ok, not technically my first Tamagotchi ever, but it is the first I've played with in two decades. I got myself a Tamagotchi Paradise after some gentle encouragement from my partner, and I finally put batteries in it this week. It's surprisingly fun!! Much less demanding of my time than I expected, too. I picked the purple shell but you can't really tell under the silicone case I bought. It offers good protection when I drop it (and boy am I good at dropping it), but I mainly got it because the toy by itself was difficult for me to comfortably hold. Having a case on makes it easier for me to wrap my hands around and press buttons. It being such a cute shape is a nice bonus too :] I've raised two Sky Tamas to adult so far and I just hatched a third in the Land field!

This is my bee son. His name is Liam Beeson and he refuses to eat pellets.
