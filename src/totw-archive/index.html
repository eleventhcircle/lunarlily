The ghosts of the things of weeks past

Wanna see some previous things of the week? You're in the right place! Entries are posted in reverse chronological order (a.k.a. most recent first). Sometime soon I need to figure out pagination so this doesn't become terrible to scroll through, but for now it's probably alright.

But wait, what's a thing of the week?

Great question! A thing of the week is some Thing I'm particularly fixated on during any given week - there's really no restriction on what the Thing can be. I thought it would be a fun way to document how my interests fluctuate over time and also encourage me to regularly check/update the site when I first launched it.

*Note: Contrary to expectations set by the name, the thing of the week is usually not updated every single week but rather when I remember or have the time/energy to. Life be happening sometimes, yknow?
